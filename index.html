MEET RAFA SEGNINI

Rafa started to feel the need for designing from the user perspective. After sharing frustrations and struggles with other colleagues in the field anytime we used for first time a new product developed for engineers.

After years working on site and the field he discovered the UI/UX world, with time a passion for highly usable products was awakened on him. He is convinced that UX/UI with a solid data analysis and statistics produce accurate measurable results, that can be gauged in the same way an engineer adjust a machine.

His ideal clients are those ones with products that can be analysed and studied before generating the first sketch. All products can be analysed and studied with the adequate technique.
